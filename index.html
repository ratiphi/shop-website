Aloha!

Shaka Vibe is the first bubble tea stand in Maui. We offer delicious fruit slushes, milk slushes, fruit tea, milk tea, shave ice, and lemonades. Shaka Vibe has the best boba on the island!

We are open 10am to 5pm everyday. Closed on Thanksgiving, Christmas day, and New Years day. Come visit one of our two Maui locations today!

Mahalo!

Where to find us.
